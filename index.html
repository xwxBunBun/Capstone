Arcane is an animated series based on the League of Legends universe, developed by Riot Games.

Set in the fictional city of Piltover and the oppressed undercity of Zaun, the show explores themes of class struggle, power dynamics, and personal ambition. It also dives into the backstories of several iconic League of Legends characters.

Main characters

Jinx/Powder

Role: Leader of Zaun

Jinx is a manic and unpredictable character who loves to create chaos, often leaving destruction in her wake.

Vi (violet)

Role: Piltover Enforcer

Vi is a tough and rebellious fighter with a strong sense of justice. She is the adoptive sister of Jinx.

Caitlyn Kirammen

Role: Piltover Enforcer

Caitlyn is a skilled marksman and the Sheriff of Piltover. She strives for justice and works closely with Vi to maintain order.

Jayce Talis

Role: Scientist

Jayce is a brilliant inventor and a skilled fighter who is committed to advancing science and protecting Piltover from threats.

Viktor

Role: Scientist

Viktor is a visionary scientist who is dedicated to improving the world, but his ideals often clash with others around him.

Heimerdinger

Role: Scientist and Professor

Heimerdinger is a brilliant, eccentric scientist who serves as a professor at the Academy of Piltover.

Silco

Role: FormerLeader of Zaun

Silco is a ruthless leader who seeks to build an independent Zaun, using manipulation and force to achieve his goals.

Vander

Role: Former Leader of Zaun

Vander is a strong and caring father figure to Vi, Jinx, Mylo & Claggor. who was once the leader of Zaun's underworld

Ekko

Role: Leader of the Firelights

Ekko is a resourceful and intelligent leader, who fights for the freedom of Zaun's oppressed people.

Isha

Role: Loyalist to Zaun

Isha is a kind and loyal character who helps Jinx & Sevika.

Mylo & Claggor

Role: Sons of Vander

Mylo and Claggor are close friends of Vi and Jinx, providing support in their chaotic lives.

Mel & Ambessa

Role: Political Figures/ Daughter & Mother

Mel and Ambessa are influential political figures in Piltover, each with their own agendas.

Sevika

Role: Zaun Enforcer

Sevika is a brutal enforcer working under Silco, known for her unwavering loyalty and strength.
